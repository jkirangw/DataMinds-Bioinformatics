Our Services

BioinformaticsDataMinds offers a wide range of services to support your research and development projects:

  • Advanced Omics Data Analysis: RNAseq, ChIP-Seq, ATAC-seq, Genome assembly, and customized analyses, including lcWGS PopGen analysis.
  • Sequencing Analysis Pipeline Development: Creation and optimization of robust sequencing analysis pipelines to streamline data processing.
  • Laboratory Sequencing Workflow Setup: Comprehensive support in setting up ONT sequencing in your lab, from DNA isolation to data quality control.
  • Specialized Training: Courses in Bash scripting, R and Python programming as well as Nextflow specifically tailored for bioinformatics applications.
