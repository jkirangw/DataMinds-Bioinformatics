About

BioinformaticsDataMinds provides scientists in academia and industry with effective bioinformatics solutions for the analysis of large-scale sequencing data. In contemporary life science research, sequencing technologies play a pivotal role. While the process of sample generation and sequencing is becoming more accessible and cost-effective, the analysis of resulting data is increasingly intricate. Skilled experts are indispensable to manage the vast amount of data, employ appropriate analytical methods, and aid in deriving meaningful insights. DataMinds Bioinformatics was established precisely to address these challenges. We bring together a distinctive team with backgrounds in life sciences and bioinformatics, employing a streamlined collaboration approach with our clients and leveraging our extensive technical expertise. Our objective is not merely to process data but to interpret it in a biological context, providing meaningful insights from your sequencing projects. Our services encompass the setup of laboratory sequencing workflows, including DNA isolation, library preparations, and long-read sequencing such as Oxford Nanopore. Additionally, we develop and implement pipelines for DNA sequence analysis.

Provides specialized short courses in R and Python programming, specifically designed for bioinformatics applications. Our expertise extends to conducting advanced omics data analyses, encompassing RNAseq, ChIP-Seq, ATAC-seq, Genome assembly, and customized analyses, including lcWGS PopGen analysis. Moreover, we excel in the development of robust sequencing analysis pipelines to optimize data processing. Additionally, we offer comprehensive support in the implementation of ONT sequencing in your laboratory, covering the entire process from DNA isolation to rigorous Data QC.

Vision

To propel transformative innovation within the life sciences industry by empowering organizations to harness the full potential of bioinformatics, driving breakthroughs in research, discovery, and healthcare.

Mission

To empower life science organizations to unlock the full potential of sequencing data, enabling them to analyze and interpret complex biological information, leading to impactful insights and discoveries.

Purpose

To democratize access to state-of-the-art bioinformatic data analysis for life science organisations, empowering them to make informed decisions and drive advancements in research, development, and healthcare.

Team

The team comprises experienced computational and experimental life scientists, each bringing a wealth of expertise to the table in constructing and fine-tuning a variety of bioinformatics solutions. We are committed to providing comprehensive support at every stage of your journey, from meticulous project planning to insightful data interpretation, ensuring unparalleled assistance and success throughout your endeavours.
